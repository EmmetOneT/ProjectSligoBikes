BMX Bikes

What you need to know.

BMX is a great form of expression. On this page, you will find everything you need to know from the bike and safety gear to tips and tricks to skate parks in Sligo. As with all the other disciplines, safety is of huge importance when riding BMX bikes so be sure to pick yourself out all of the gear you may need to express yourself safely.
There are different styles of BMX. Freestyle, flatland and racing will all require different safety gear to ride safely.

Skateparks in Sligo

Zero Gravity skatepark has plenty of amenities for skaters and BMXers aline. Theres a fee payable in the reception. The park includes a hubba, curbs, roll-ins, jump boxes, bowl, quarterpipes, ledges, banks, quarterpipes and a miniramp. It also has a skateschool, a skateshop and a bar.
